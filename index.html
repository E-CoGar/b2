About Us

The UN Sustainable Development Goals on which we based ourselves to create this product were 2, which are 11, which are sustainable cities and communities, which deals with the fact that the world population is constantly increasing and we need to build modern and sustainable cities. For all of us to survive and thrive with a good future and quality of life, we need new smart urban planning that creates safe, affordable and resilient cities with green living conditions. And we also base ourselves on goal number 13, which is climate action and talks about the big problem today, which is climate change, which is a real and undeniable threat to all of our civilization. The effects are already visible in all parts of the world and will be catastrophic unless we act now. Through education, innovation and compliance with climate commitments, the necessary changes can be made to protect the planet and its integrity. Our mission is to be able to help combat climate change in a very simple way where we can all contribute day by day.
